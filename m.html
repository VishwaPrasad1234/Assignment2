About Us

SRK International School is dedicated to nurturing future leaders who are equipped to succeed in a competitive world. By combining academic excellence with a focus on character building and skill development, the school aims to empower its students to achieve their full potential and make meaningful contributions to society.SRK International School was founded to provide quality education and holistic development. Starting with classes up to Grade 3, it expanded yearly and now offers education up to the senior secondary level. Affiliated with CBSE, the school ensures a strong academic foundation alongside overall student growth.

Subjects

Computer Science is the study of computers, computational systems, and programming. It involves learning how software and applications are designed, developed, and maintained. Students explore topics such as algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, databases, and networking. The field helps solve real-world problems using technology and plays an important role in modern industries and innovation.
Biology is the scientific study of living organisms, including plants, animals, and microorganisms. It examines life processes such as growth, reproduction, metabolism, evolution, and interaction with the environment. Students learn about different branches like genetics, ecology, microbiology, and human biology, which help in understanding life and solving problems related to health, agriculture, and the environment.
Commerce is the field that deals with business activities such as trade, finance, accounting, marketing, and economics. It helps students understand how businesses operate, how goods and services are exchanged, and how financial systems function. The subject also covers entrepreneurship, banking, taxation, and management, preparing students for careers in business and the corporate sector.
The History Department focuses on the study of past events, civilizations, cultures, and historical developments across different periods of time. It helps students understand how societies, governments, economies, and traditions evolved. By analyzing historical sources and events, students learn critical thinking, research skills, and gain insight into how the past shapes the present and future.
Mathematics is the branch of knowledge that deals with numbers, quantities, shapes, patterns, and logical reasoning. It helps us understand relationships between different elements and solve problems in science, engineering, technology, and everyday life. Mathematics includes areas such as arithmetic, algebra, geometry, calculus, and statistics, and it is widely used to analyze data, make predictions, and model real-world situations.
